1. How it works:

  1. Everyone loves Devine foods, and as a token of gratitude for your loyal support, we will give you points to the equivalent of 5% of your total bill (excluding VAT and waitron tip).
  2. You can build up your balance over a period of time until you reach 1000 points.
  3. On reaching 1000 points, which amounts to a total spend of R1 000 (excluding VAT and waitron tip), Devine will automatically load a R50 (Fifty Rand) voucher to your Devine Rewards profile.
  4. You will be notified via email / SMS that the voucher has been loaded to your Devine Rewards profile.
  5. As a member, you will also be notified about our awesome promotions and any other exclusive opportunities.
  6. You will not be able to earn points or redeem vouchers if your Panarottis Rewards profile has not been registered.

2. To Join/ Register on the Panarottis Rewards Programme:

  1. Download the Devine Rewards App from Google Play or Apple App Store to apply for a digital Devine Rewards profile, alternatively visit www.devinefoods.co.za and apply.
  2. You can also ask your waitron for a physical card and register your card online www.devinefoods.co.za
  3. You can start accumulating points immediately after registration.
